Arc

Welcome to Arc.

Arc is an intelligent personal assistant designed to help users plan and optimize their daily tasks with maximum efficiency.

Users can input their tasks, preferred locations, time windows, and priorities. Arc then generates an optimized schedule by calculating the shortest routes, minimizing travel time, and organizing tasks in the most efficient order. It also suggests parallel tasking when possible and alerts users if their schedule is unrealistic.

Arc also provides smart recommendations by suggesting everything you need for each task — from essential documents to small but important items — ensuring you are fully prepared.

It integrates with your email, detects tasks and appointments, and can assist in replying using AI.

Arc helps you stay organized, save time, and reduce stress.
